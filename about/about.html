ABOUT

Hi! I am Ming Yuan. Currently, I am a student in Singapore!


One of the things I love to do is programming! Seizing the opportunity the Pandemic has given, I started to learn how to code, starting off with HTML and CSS. Not only that, I've had the chance to build projects with Micro:bit MakeCode! Currently, I am learning front-end JavaScript, and I hope to do cooler projects as I progress!


I also love capturing moments on my camera. Starting Photography as a CCA in 2016, it has become one of my favourite pastimes, and I have cultivated my own unique visual style. Through my photos, I hope to evoke deep thought in the viewer, and leave them something meaningful. Do check out my work @ming.photo!
