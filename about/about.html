ABOUT

Hi! I am Ming Yuan. I graduated from Victoria Junior College, and I'm currently an intern at TinkerTanker, and volunteering at Tzu Chi.


I am passionate about all things STEAM! I was the Electronics Head in VJC Robotics and Makers Club, and this allowed me to embark on engineering projects such as a remote-operated submarine and a RC fixed-wing aircraft, for our participation in SAFMC 2022. I also worked on a Science Research Project that was presented at SSEF 2022 and the International Elementz Fair 2022! During my free time, I work on personal projects, such as this website, and try to deepen my expertise in engineering through online courses, such as the FreeCodeCamp Responsive Web Design course. I am planning on learning more JavaScript, Python and Arduino, and use that knowledge to build cooler projects.

I am also a freelance student photographer, having taken photos for events such as National School Games 2022, MusicFest and VJC Concert Series, VS Speech Day, and may more. I have had close to 6 years of experience, not just covering events on my own, but also leading a team of photographers to do so. I mainly take event, street, sports and landscape photography. You can check out my work@ming.photo , and for any inquiries or collaborations, do feel free to contact me!
