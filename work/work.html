Ocean Trash Cleanup Device

The ocean trash cleanup device is aimed at removing buoyant & surface trash. It works by using a pressure gradient generated by a 9V water pump. This helps pull less dense objects into a bin, for future disposal. It works as a stationary bin attached to harbours and piers or if possible, can be used as attachments on boats.

This is a prototype built with recyclable materials.

Features:
  1. Current drawing pump which pulls buoyant trash into the bin
  2. Rotatable lid which allow for the opening and closing of the bin
  3. LED screen displaying the status of the bin (Operational/Full)
  4. Manual Control with two buttons
  5. Button A: lid close
  6. Button B: lid open
  7. Bluetooth activated control with smartphone

Automated Lights and Attendance IoT System

Ever used Google Home to turn on the lights? Well... what if we made our own?

Introducing, the automated lights and attendance system! This IoT device detects entry and exit through PIR sensors, logging the data onto the ThingSpeak IoT platform. A telegram message will thus be sent to the user through IFTTT indicating whether a person has entered or left the room, and the total number of people in the room.

Features:
  1. Automated lights
    • Automatically turn on when people enter the room
    • Automatically turn off when there is no one in the room

  2. Attendance system
    • Able to record the number of people in the room at a specific time!
    • Able to record the number of people in the room at a specific time!

  3. Can view the number of people in the room in real time!
    • Notifications when someone enters/leaves the room
Parts:
  1. Octopus 5mm LED brick OBLED
  2. 2x AM412 PIR sensor*
  3. Micro:bit V1*
  4. IoT:bit breakout board*

*These parts can be found in the ELECFREAKS Micro:bit Smart Science IoT Kit.
